is a cross-platform mobile app built with **Flutter & Dart**, designed to combine the **Bible experience** with **faith-based community and local connection**.  
Users can:
- Read and bookmark Bible verses  
- Join faith-based communities (Catholic, Protestant, Anglican, Mormon, etc.)  
- Discover nearby faith events and get notifications  
- Access and purchase religious merchandise through the app  

---

Tech Stack

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|----------|
| **Flutter / Dart** | Frontend mobile app (Android & iOS) |
| **MySQL** | Backend database for users, posts, and Bible content |
| **Figma** | UI/UX design and prototyping |
| **Firebase (optional)** | Authentication, cloud messaging, and push notifications |
| **Node.js / Express (optional)** | Backend API layer connecting Flutter with MySQL |

---

Core Features

### 1. Bible Reading
- Read the entire Bible (offline/online)
- Highlight and save favorite verses
- Daily verse notifications

### 2. Faith Communities
- Join or create groups based on denomination
- Post reflections, questions, or testimonies
- Like, comment, and interact with others

### 3. Marketplace
- Browse and buy religious merchandise (crosses, rosaries, shirts, mugs, etc.)
- Merch sellers can create mini-shops in the app
- Secure checkout (via mobile money or PayPal)
- Marketplace Spotlight: promote trusted sellers within the faith community

### 4. Faith Events & Notifications
- Discover upcoming church events, youth meetups, or prayer sessions
- Get notifications about events based on your denomination or location
- RSVP and share events within your community

### 5. Personal Profile
- Add your denomination, favorite verses, and prayer goals
- View activity feed and saved posts
- Connect with other believers

---

How FaithConnect Stands Out
Unlike traditional Bible apps (like YouVersion or Bible.is), **FaithConnect** focuses on **community, culture, and connection**:
- **Community:** Faith-based networking and discussion, not just reading
- **Events:** Connect users to real-world gatherings and activities
- **Commerce:** Help small faith-based brands sell directly through the app
- **Localization:** Designed for African audiences (starting in Rwanda), blending culture and spirituality
- **Offline Bible Access:** Lightweight and optimized for low internet environments
